About me

I grew up in a small village on the countryside of France with barely enough internet connection to browse the internet.

One Christmas, my dad bought my brother and I a Nintendo Gamecube (yes, I was a Nintendo kid) and that sparked my interest for videogames. After years of playing on different consoles, and later on, on PC, I started to wonder what it was like on the "other side".

I started exploring game development on my own in my teenage years with softwares like RPG Maker, FPS Creator and Unity 5. At the time though, Youtube tutorials were still pretty scarce and the task of learning it all on my own was too daunting so I waited until college to seriously start to dabble into it.

I gained a pretty strong interest in programming, and I eventually got very fond of coding the core mechanics of a game, enough to want to turn it into my full-time job. A few years and projects later, here I am ! Nice to meet you !
